Exotic Kosher Birds

Wild and Game Birds which are accepted as kosher in New York.



  • Common Street Pigeon (yonah)
  • Laughing Doves (turtle dove)
  • Ring Neck Dove (turtle dove)
  • Sparrow (tzipor dror)
  • Mourning Dove
  • Hungarian Partridge
  • Red Legged Partridge
  • Chukar Partridge
  • Philby Partridge
  • Barbary Partridge
  • Wild and Domestic turkey
