About Us

להבדיל בין הטהור ובין הטמא

The Biblical Ornithological Society and the Vaad Mishmeres Misores HaOf documents the birds for which there is a recognized
מסורה tradition of permissibility. Unless otherwise mentioned, all the birds mentioned on these pages are accepted as kosher in New York.
As per the accepted psak a bird can only be consumed if the person is confident that they can properly identify the bird.
Since Talmudic times (חלין ס''ג) people have misidentified kosher species. Despite the names and pictures on this website no
bird should be consumed unless the shochet is confident in their ability to accurately identify the bird they are shechting.
