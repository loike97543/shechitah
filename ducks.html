Kosher Ducks

As per the Rama (YD 82:3) ducks and other waterfowl are only consumed if there is a valid mesorah.
These are the ducks that are accepted as kosher in New York. In general the kosher ducks are mallard derived.
The minhag in New York is to avoid the Muscovy duck and all ducks derived from the Muscovy duck.
